Babaylanes, Inc.

Babaylanes, Inc. is a Philippine-based organization that promotes LGBTQI+ rights and gender equality. It supports LGBTQI+ communities through education, advocacy, and network-building. The group offers programs on SOGIESC awareness, leadership training, and policy support to help create a more equal and inclusive society.
